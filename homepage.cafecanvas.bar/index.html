THE WORKFLOW

How CafeCanva Works

Six steps. One platform. Complete operational visibility across every role in your business.

01

Admin Sets Up

Owner builds the menu, floor plan, staff accounts, and tax config in Store Admin.

02

Staff Take Orders

Waiters tap a green table, browse the menu, select modifiers, and fire the order to kitchen.

03

Kitchen Gets Tickets

KDS instantly shows the new KOT. Chefs mark items Pending → Preparing → Ready.

04

Food is Served

Ready items notify the serving waiter. Marked Served — table status updates live.

05

Payment Collected

One-tap bill generation. CGST/SGST auto-applied. Cash, Card, or Razorpay UPI QR.

06

Analytics Update

Owner's dashboard reflects new revenue, order count, and occupancy in real-time.
