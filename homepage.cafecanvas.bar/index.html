How CafeCanva Works

1
⚙️

Admin Set Up

Create your menu, modifiers, floor plans, and staff profiles inside Store Admin.

2
📱

Staff Take Orders

Waiters select a table, select modifier options, and place orders directly on the tablet.

3
👨🍳

Kitchen Prep

Chefs see KOTs in the KDS and update cooking status: Preparing → Ready.

4
🍽️

Food is Served

Waiters get a sound notification alert, serve the fresh order, and mark it Served.

5
💳

Auto Billing

Cashier prints invoices with auto taxes and takes payments via UPI QR or Cash.

6
📊

Live Analytics

Owner's metrics update instantly: daily sales, transaction counts, and inventory status.
